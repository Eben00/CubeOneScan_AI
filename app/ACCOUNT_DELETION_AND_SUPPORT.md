# Account Deletion and Support Procedure

## Support Channels
- Support email: [support@yourdomain.com]
- Privacy/deletion email: [privacy@yourdomain.com]
- Support SLA target: [e.g., 2 business days]

## Account Deletion Workflow (Operational)
1. User submits deletion request via support or privacy email.
2. Verify requester identity (email ownership + secondary check).
3. Mark request ticket with timestamp and requester details.
4. Remove or anonymize user account in auth backend.
5. Remove or anonymize associated operational records where required.
6. Confirm completion to user and record audit trail.

## Recommended Timelines
- Acknowledge request: within 48 hours.
- Complete deletion: within 30 days (or jurisdiction requirement).

## Minimum Audit Record
- Request ID
- Request date/time
- User identity reference
- Actions performed
- Completion date/time
- Operator name

## Play Console Requirement
Ensure account deletion method is visible to users in:
- app listing support details
- privacy policy
- any in-app help/settings section (recommended)
